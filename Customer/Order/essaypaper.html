Do you format papers in MLA style?

Yes, we do format papers in MLA style. 
There are many different ways to cite the sources in an academic paper.  The citation style depends on the subject or topic being discussed.  
For example:
APA (American Psychological Association) is used by Education, Psychology, and Sciences
MLA (Modern Language Association) style is used by the Humanities
Chicago/Turabian style is generally used by Business, History, and the Fine Arts
